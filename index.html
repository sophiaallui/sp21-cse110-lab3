Anyway, I also made a smoothie bowl ‚ú®

This is how I made it:

  1. Buy frozen fruits and orange juice (would recommend to buy both from Costco!!)
  2. Get your blender and add 2/3 full with fruits, and from the amount of fruits you added, pour 1/2 of orange juice from that amount. Basically, add less orange juice, about 1:2 ratio of orange juice to frozen fruits.
  3. BLEND! BLEND! BLEND! You want it to be thick so it can be similar to an acai bowl!
  4. Pour your smoothie into a bowl, cut up some fruits you have around, and add other toppings like coconut flakes, almonds, chia seeds, and etc...
